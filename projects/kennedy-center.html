Toy Variety Pack Package Design

Designed a seasonal event pamphlet for the Kennedy Center’s 2024–25 Fall/Winter program in an Editorial Design course, applying typographic hierarchy, layout systems, and visual consistency to produce a polished, client-ready print collateral piece.

  • Context: Editorial Design (Class Project)
  • Timeframe: 1 Month (Sept - Oct 2024)

Approach

My approach was to create a clear, inviting layout that organized the Kennedy Center’s fall/winter programming into something accessible and visually grounded—highlighting the energy of each event while keeping the information effortless to navigate.
