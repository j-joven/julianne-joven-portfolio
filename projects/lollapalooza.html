Lollapalooza Rebrand

Developed a comprehensive rebrand for Lollapalooza in a Brand Identity Design course, including a redesigned logo, stationery system, mockups, and a detailed branding guidelines booklet to establish a cohesive visual identity across print and digital touchpoints.

  • Context: Brand Identity Design (Class Project)
  • Timeframe: 3 Months (Feb-May 2025)

Approach

My approach was to rebuild Lollapalooza’s identity into something more cohesive and contemporary without losing the festival’s playful edge.
