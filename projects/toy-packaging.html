Toy Variety Pack Package Design

Designed packaging for a collectible toy set in a Package Design course featuring original felt character keychains, creating the Mimoka & Friends brand identity and a colorful, playful box inspired by contemporary blind-box collectibles with a clear display window.

  • Context: Package Design (Class Project)
  • Timeframe: 1.5 Months (Late Oct-Early Dec 2025)

Approach

My approach was to build a cohesive packaging experience that highlights each character’s unique tone while maintaining a unified “Cosmic Parade” identity that feels vibrant, modern, and collectible.
